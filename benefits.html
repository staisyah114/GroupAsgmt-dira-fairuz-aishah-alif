Benefits

The quality of the air we breathe has a direct impact on our health. Our mask protects against microscopic particles caused by air pollution, dust, smoke, allergens, biologics, and natural causes. Wellness is the result of conscientiously applied healthy living principles, which begin with breathing clean air. Our mask is effective at protecting against viral and bacterial airborne particles.


Pollen particles that enter the respiratory system can range in size from 3 microns to 100 microns. We are currently seeing an increase in allergen sensitivity. When pollen counts are high and in environments with triggering particles and odours, wearing our mask is a non-invasive way to protect your lungs from allergen inhalation.


Our mask is extremely effective at keeping known hazardous particles out of the respiratory system, particularly in dusty, mouldy, polluted, and crowded environments.
